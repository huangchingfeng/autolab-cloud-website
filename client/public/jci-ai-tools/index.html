💡 Coach Feng's AI Principles
阿峰老师的 AI 心法

  • 你是机长，AI 是机组人员
    You are the Captain, AI is your crew — you make decisions, AI executes
  • 不要只用一个工具，要串起来
    Don't use just one tool — chain them: ChatGPT drafts → Perplexity researches → Gamma presents
  • 先从免费版开始
    Start with free versions — upgrade only when you outgrow them
  • 每天用 10 分钟：会用、懂用、好用、每天用
    Use AI 10 mins daily — habit beats skill. Learn it, know it, love it, use it daily!
  • 给 AI 好的指令
    Give AI great prompts — tell it who you are, what you need, who it's for, and the style
